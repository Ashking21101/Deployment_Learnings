Q: What is your return policy?
A: You can return any product within 30 days of delivery as long as the tag is intact.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard delivery takes 4-6 business days. Express delivery takes 1-2 business days.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: After your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking ID and a tracking link.

Q: Do you offer exchanges?
A: Yes, we offer exchanges for size and color. Exchange pickup is free.

Q: What payment methods do you support?
A: We support credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and Cash on Delivery.
